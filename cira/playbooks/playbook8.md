# Playbook 8: Investment & Crypto Fraud

**Categories Covered:** Demat Account Fraud, Cryptocurrency Crime

---

## 1. Immediate Steps (Stop the Damage & Recover Fast)

### Demat/Trading Account Fraud (unauthorized trades, fake investment apps/advisors):
1. **Stop all further payments immediately** to the "advisor"/platform — legitimate brokers never guarantee fixed high returns or pressure urgent deposits.
2. **Log in to your actual Demat/trading account** (via official broker app/website, not a link sent by the advisor) and check for unauthorized trades or withdrawals.
3. **Freeze/lock your Demat account** by contacting your broker (Zerodha, Groww, Upstox, etc.) and your Depository Participant (NSDL/CDSL) immediately if you see unauthorized activity.
4. **Change your trading account password and enable 2FA** right away.
5. **Stop sharing your trading account login/OTP** with anyone, including "relationship managers" or "advisors" who reach out unsolicited.
6. **If you invested via a fake app** (not your real broker's official app), uninstall it and do not enter any more banking details into it.

### Crypto Fraud (fake exchanges, rug pulls, pig-butchering scams, fraudulent crypto "investment" schemes):
1. **Stop sending any more crypto/money immediately** — common patterns include fake profit dashboards showing huge gains to lure more deposits, then blocking withdrawals.
2. **Do not pay any "withdrawal fee," "tax," or "unlocking charge"** demanded before releasing your funds — this is a classic follow-up scam stage.
3. **Take screenshots of your wallet balance/dashboard** showing the fraudulent platform's claimed holdings before the site/app disappears.
4. **Note down the wallet address(es)** you sent crypto to — this is the single most important piece of information for tracing on the blockchain.
5. **If you shared your seed phrase/private key with anyone or any site**, immediately move any remaining legitimate crypto to a new wallet with a freshly generated seed phrase — your old wallet is compromised forever once the seed phrase is exposed.
6. **Revoke token approvals/permissions** if you connected your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to a suspicious dApp/site — use a tool like Etherscan's "Token Approval Checker" to revoke access.

## 2. Evidence Storage (What to Save & Why)

| Evidence Type | What to Capture |
|---|---|
| **Investment platform/app details** | Screenshots of the app/website, company name claimed, registration/license claims made |
| **Communication records** | Chats/calls with the "advisor," promises of guaranteed returns, pressure tactics used |
| **Payment proof** | Bank transfer/UPI receipts, transaction IDs, amounts and dates of every deposit made |
| **Crypto wallet addresses** | The exact wallet address(es) funds were sent to — copy the full address, don't paraphrase |
| **Transaction hash (TXID)** | The blockchain transaction ID for each transfer — allows tracing on blockchain explorers (Etherscan, BscScan, etc.) |
| **Fake dashboard screenshots** | Screenshot the fraudulent platform's shown balance/profits before it disappears or you lose access |
| **Demat/broker account statements** | Download official statements showing unauthorized trades if applicable |
| **Timeline log** | Date of first contact, every deposit made, when withdrawal was refused, when you realized it was a scam |

**Tip:** For crypto fraud, **save the wallet address and transaction hash above everything else** — these allow investigators to trace fund movement even when the platform itself disappears.

## 3. Reporting (In Priority Order)

### Step 1 — Contact Your Broker/Bank Immediately (if payment is recent)
- For Demat fraud: Call your broker and Depository Participant (NSDL/CDSL) to freeze the account.
- For bank-routed crypto payments: Call your bank to attempt a transaction reversal if very recent.

### Step 2 — Dial 1930 (National Cyber Crime Financial Fraud Helpline)
- Report immediately — especially effective if the fraud involved a bank/UPI transfer that can still be traced/frozen before withdrawal.

### Step 3 — File a Complaint on the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
- **[cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)** — File under "Financial Fraud." Include wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and all platform details — this portal has a dedicated category for crypto-related fraud reporting.

### Step 4 — Report to SEBI (for Demat/stock market fraud)
- **SEBI SCORES Portal:** [scores.gov.in](https://scores.gov.in) — Securities and Exchange Board of India's official complaint portal for fraudulent brokers, fake advisors, or unauthorized Demat account trades.
- Also verify if the "advisor" was SEBI-registered via [SEBI's registered intermediary list](https://www.sebi.gov.in) — report if they falsely claimed registration.

### Step 5 — Report to FIU-IND (for large-scale/organized crypto fraud)
- **Financial Intelligence Unit – India:** [fiuindia.gov.in](https://fiuindia.gov.in) — relevant for large-scale money laundering-linked crypto fraud cases.

### Step 6 — File FIR at Local Police Cyber Cell / Economic Offences Wing
- Strongly recommended for investment fraud, as Economic Offences Wings (EOW) in most cities specifically handle these cases and can request blockchain analytics support.

## 4. Helpful Resources

| Need | Resource |
|---|---|
| **Verify SEBI-registered brokers/advisors** | [sebi.gov.in](https://www.sebi.gov.in) — "Investor" section has a search tool for registered intermediaries |
| **Trace crypto transactions** | [Etherscan](https://etherscan.io) (Ethereum), [BscScan](https://bscscan.com) (Binance Smart Chain), or the relevant blockchain's public explorer — paste the TXID/wallet address to track fund movement |
| **Revoke malicious wallet approvals** | [Etherscan Token Approval Checker](https://etherscan.io/tokenapprovalchecker) or [revoke.cash](https://revoke.cash) |
| **Check crypto exchange legitimacy** | Look for FIU-IND registration on the exchange's website footer — verify directly on [fiuindia.gov.in](https://fiuindia.gov.in) |
| **RBI/SEBI investor awareness on scams** | [rbi.org.in](https://www.rbi.org.in) and [sebi.gov.in](https://www.sebi.gov.in) investor education sections |

## 5. Quick Checklist Summary

1. ✅ Stop sending any more money/crypto immediately
2. ✅ Freeze your Demat account / move remaining crypto to a new wallet if seed phrase was exposed
3. ✅ Save wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and all payment proof
4. ✅ Dial 1930 → file complaint on cybercrime.gov.in
5. ✅ Report to SEBI SCORES (Demat/stock fraud) or FIU-IND (large crypto fraud)
6. ✅ File FIR with local Economic Offences Wing for investigation support
7. ✅ Revoke any wallet permissions given to suspicious dApps
